    I am currently a third-year master student with the school of Computer Science and Information Engineering, Hefei University of Technology (HFUT), supervised by Prof. Zhao Zhang. I am also a member of Lab for Multimedia Computing (LMC) led by Prof. Meng Wang and Prof. Richang Hong. I received the bachelor degree from Department of Mathematics, Northwest Agriculture and Forestry University (NWAFU). I am interested in image/video restoration and enhancement.
